We want to encourage you to grow stronger in the knowledge of Christ as we invite you to share these truths with others. May the God of glory empower you to face the future without fear. May God himself keep you blameless unto the coming of our LORD and Savior, Jesus-Christ, and the catching up of his glorious Church on the last day of this age.

    3. About Us

We are an apostolic group who believes the one God of the Bible is Jesus Christ. We believe salvation only comes by obedience to the biblical message preached at Pentecost; The death, burial, and resurrection of the Lord Jesus-Christ leading to the command given by Peter in Acts 2:38. We believe there is one baptism (Ephesians 4:4-5), which must be administered by total immersion, calling on the name of the Lord Jesus-Christ for the remission of sins (Acts 8:16, 10:48, 19:5, 22:16). We also believe in the necessity of the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in other tongues (Acts 2:4) as the seal of salvation. Now if any man has not the Spirit of Christ, he is none of his (Romans 8:9).
We also firmly believe that the true church will be on earth for the entire 7 years of tribulation at the end of this age. On the last day, we believe the Lord himself shall descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of the archangel, and with the trump of God: and the dead in Christ shall rise first: Then we which are alive and remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds, to meet the Lord in the air: and so shall we ever be with the Lord. (1 Thes 4:16-17)
“End Of the Age” is an edification platform focussing on the fulfillment of Bible prophecies through the development of current events. The concept came into existence first and foremost from divine intervention in the lives of active members of this group. We will not go into the many details of how God did prepare each of the members, but none of them had studied prophecies in depth. As a matter of fact, we all knew very little about end-time prophecies until a turning point took place.

    4. The call

It all began in December 2019, when God intervened in Brother Gerry A. Ripley’s life in a supernatural way.  Having been in the ministry full time as a foreign missionary, Gerry was not a novice with the word of God and he knew what it was like to depend on God for everything. He was well acquainted with the presence of the Lord, having been baptized with the Holy Spirit since 1995. However, this unusual encounter with God had a very specific purpose; A new call in the prophetic ministry for the last days. The wickedness of men has reached a point of no return and God has determined that He is ending the age. 
Brother Ripley began a very intensive training during which he was brought in supernatural experiences with God and in the spiritual world. The training lasted approximately 18 months. It was a time of learning and preparing for his new assignment. During that time he wrote a full commentary on the entire book of Revelation with accuracy such as was never seen before. You will find his rock-solid interpretations of prophecies are the most relevant confirmation we have that his calling to this end-time ministry came directly from our heavenly father. God wants to bring the correct truths of Bible prophecy to the true Church of the New Covenant (Acts 2:38) and to the world.
Although there were obstacles right from the start with the dissemination of this important message, brother Ripley began to teach and broadcast this end-time message on the Facebook platform. Being met with strong opposition, mostly from church leadership, two Facebook groups ended up being shut down as a result of too many complaints. A third Facebook group was created under the name: "APOCALYPSE OF JESUS-CHRIST''. Many members had stopped following Gerry's teachings and left the groups for various reasons. Nevertheless, the group stays in operation to this day and continues to grow. 
Gerry knew from the beginning of this ministry that he was going to need assistance, help, and support in order to follow through with the assignment he received from God.  In spring 2020, just a few months after the lockdowns, God drew brother Michel Veilleux’s attention to the book of Revelation and revealed to him that the pre-tribulation rapture doctrine was impossible and unbiblical. Within one month, Michel received a Facebook invitation from Gerry who then invited him to join Gerry's group on Bible prophecies. They had no previous acquaintance with each other, but Michel quickly learned and understood the revelations brother Gerry had received from God. Since the Lord had already taught brother Michel in 2001 to reject any influence coming from dispensationalism and receive true Covenant theology, he willingly joined his efforts with brother Gerry’s work in this ministry. God showed His approval by granting more revelations and accuracy in the understanding of end-time prophecies. 
Brother Ripley had also felt that a website would eventually be necessary to move forward with the tremendous responsibility of bringing the book of Revelation back to where it belongs; in the Church of the last days. That’s who it was written for. As Brother Gerry prayed earnestly for a website, the Lord promised him he would send someone with the knowledge and the capacities needed to build a website.
Approximately two years later in June 2021, a post from Apocalypse of Jesus Christ ended up in a place where God drew brother James Chuitcheu’s attention. James is a young man with a great passion for the truth. He immediately felt the spirit of truth and joined the Facebook group, “Apocalypse of Jesus Christ”. It wasn’t long before he put his talents into the service of God’s purpose in this ministry. He translated the entire commentary into French and then proceeded with the creation of this website with the help of other members mentioned below.
