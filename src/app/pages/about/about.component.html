About

    1. Preambule

Beloved, dear readers, the signs of the end of time are felt day by day and are visibly evident when you pay attention. Indeed, you have certainly made the observation yourself of the phenomena announced by Jesus in the book of Matthew 24 verses 6 to 14 (Bible). We talk about endless wars, conflicts between nations, economic crises responsible for famines, plagues, the most recent of which is the Covid-19 pandemic, natural disasters, false Christs, and false prophets seducing many people, the excessive growth of iniquity and depravity of man in all his form etc. Yes, this generation saw the confirmation of the prophecies announced in advance by the Lord Jesus, who also gave him this honorable mention: “Likewise, when you see these things, know that the Son of man is near, at the door. I tell you the truth, this generation will not pass away, unless the Son of man appears in his great glory ".
Thus, as servants of Jesus Christ we are more interested in those revelations which, first announced by the Lord, were confirmed by his holy apostles, who testified to the word of God by the virtue of the Holy -Spirit and testimony of Jesus Christ as described in the book of Revelation.

    2. Our mission

Our mission is to guide men and women of the Church of Jesus-Christ (New covenant of Acts 2:38) around the world to a deeper understanding of end-time, prophetic truth so that they will be ready for the soon coming of Jesus Christ. We believe many are searching to know with certainty how, when, and where the end-time events of Daniel, Revelation and others prophetic books will take place. We consider it as a privilege to have received all these revelations by God himself through his servant, the brother Gerry A. Ripley and we expect to guide you to the exact knowledge of Biblical truth and the end times prophecies and encourage you to grow stronger in your knowledge of Christ, inspire you to share these truths with others, and empower you to face the future unafraid. Our prayer is that you will be ready to witness and to face the period of the greatest seduction that this world has ever known from its beginning before the coming of our LORD and Savior, Jesus- Christ and the rapture of his glorious Church.

    3. About Us

“End Of the Age” is an edification platform on the revelations of end times events. The concept was born because of several decades of serving the Lord in missions for the cause of the Gospel, of a deep interest in the kingdom of God, of revelations received from the Lord and attested by the holy scriptures, experiences and observations made in the world today.
We are an apostolic group from Acts 2:38, who believe in one God whose name is Jesus Christ, in one baptism which must be by total immersion by calling on the name of the Lord Jesus, and in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with as proof the spoken in tongues. We also firmly believe that the true church will spend the 7 years of tribulation at the end of this age on this earth and will be raptured at the end of the 7 years when the Lord Jesus coming to the air in our atmosphere.
Brother Gerry A. Ripley was born in July 1965. He has 22 years of full-time ministry in the work of God. After having been a missionary outside his home country for many years to speak the word of God, he settled in the Philippines. one day in December 2019, he begins the prophetic ministry based on a direct call (actual encounter) from God to bring the correct truths of Bible prophecy to the true Church (Acts 2:38) and to the world.
Having faced difficulties when the dissemination of this precious message received from God, he created a Facebook page called `` APOCALYPSE OF JESUS-CHRIST '' inside which he began to broadcast this message of the end of the time of utmost importance. Later, he will be helped in his ministry by Brother Michel Veilleux whose Lord had also called before by bringing him out of dispensationalism to focus on the doctrine of covenants.
Gerry A. Ripley, however, believed that a Facebook page would not be enough to reach a large community, so he began to pray to the Lord to allow him to have an internet platform to help him in his ministry. However, God uplifted it by placing in the heart of a young person, Brother James CHUITCHEU IV who believed in his call and in his ministry to help him in the establishment of this website. it was not the combination of circumstances, but the true hand of God acting in the invisible to unite us to accomplish this great mission of the end of time. We hope that this web platform will achieve the desired goal by the grace of God.
